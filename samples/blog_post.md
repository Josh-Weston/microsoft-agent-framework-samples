Below we distill the most important points from the official product summary, so you can decide in seconds whether this card’s insurance is a perfect fit for your lifestyle.

## 3. How to Make a Claim

1. **Report ASAP** – Call the Allianz Global Assistance Operations Centre as soon as you notice a loss or damage.  
2. **Collect the Forms** – Once you contact them, you’ll receive claim forms (online or mailed).  
3. **Submit Documentation** – Payment receipts, police reports, damaged‑item photos, medical bills, etc.  
4. **Deadline** – Quebec residents must report within 30 days of the incident, submit documents within 90 days. Claims are usually paid within 60 days of full documentation.

> **Contact info**  
> - *Allianz Ops Centre* (Canada only, toll‑free): **1‑877‑704‑0341**  
> - *International* (collect): **1‑519‑741‑0782**  
> - Online portal: [file a claim](https://allianz-assistance.ca/en_CA/customers/file-a-claim.html)

## 4. What You Can’t Get

| Coverage | Exclusions (excerpt) |

| Medical | Pre‑existing conditions that aren’t “stable”, elective surgeries, pregnancy complications, high‑risk sports |
| Car Rental | Trucks, off‑road vehicles, motorcycles, use of rental vehicle outside Canada, day‑time speed contests |
| Purchase Security | Money, animals, consumables, tools that are used for work, items already under warranty |

## 5. Cancellation Rules

* The insurance **cannot** be cancelled independently.  
* If you cancel or close your BMO CashBack World Elite Mastercard, the insurance ends.  
* The policy will also terminate if Allianz or BMO says so, or if your card privileges are revoked/suspended.
